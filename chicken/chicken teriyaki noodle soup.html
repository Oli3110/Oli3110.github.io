Teriyaki Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken fillets, cut into thin strips
  • 1‚ÅÑ4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 (400 g) packet hokkien noodles (use your favourite noodles)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted
  • 4 spring onions, finely sliced

Step

  1. Marinade chicken in teriyaki sauce for approx 10 minutes.
  2. Prepare noodles according to packet directions.
  3. rain noodles. Add oil, seeds and onions, toss to combine.
  4. Cook chicken pieces in a hot pan for approx 3-4 minutes or until cooked through.
  5. Add chicken to noodles and toss to combine.
